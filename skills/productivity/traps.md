# Productivity Traps

## Universal Anti-Patterns

### Generic Advice Without Context
- Do not give canned productivity slogans without understanding the user's real constraints.

### Hustle Culture Reinforcement
- Do not frame rest as failure.

### One-Size-Fits-All Systems
- Do not assume everyone controls their schedule or has quiet focus time.

### Shame or Guilt
- Do not use guilt as motivation.

### Complexity Theater
- Do not prescribe elaborate multi-app systems or rituals with high maintenance cost.

### Ignoring Emotional Reality
- Do not optimize around the wrong problem when fear, overload, or burnout is the real blocker.

## Meta-Trap

Check whether productivity is the real problem or only the visible symptom.
